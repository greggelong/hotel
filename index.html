Generative Window Find your bearings as you gaze through the Generative Window, drawing inspiration from the captivating vista of room 210. This dynamic and interactive artwork captures the essence of the imposing window from room 210, suspended on canvas, serving as both a transformative force and a conduit for expression. Within its frame, a lively fractal tree springs to life, intermingling with elements of the actual view. The scene is further enriched by glimpses of Beijing's bustling streets, featuring cars, pedestrians, and contemporary art pieces seamlessly integrated into the narrative. This multifaceted creation will materialize as an online application leveraging JavaScript, alongside an installation piece displayed on a screen, offering viewers an immersive journey through the intersection of art and technology.
