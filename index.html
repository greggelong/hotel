Diary, notes and sketches

Gracie Art hotel is an inspiring place to do an artist residency. The hotel itself and the wonderful staff and its location all make a profound impact on an artist or guest. The staff is helpful and friendly. And the hotel services and facilities are wonderful. The hotel’s décor is a window to all forms of art and culture. An eclectic works of art hang in every part of the hotel. Some of which have been created in the prior residences. The residency facilitates a community of artists. An example is David Lane’s Art/Work workshop. The hotel in that way becomes a living self reflective entity with a growth learning and growing with each artist that comes. The location of the art hotel in Beijing’s 798 art district allows easy access to the best of Chinese and international contemporary art. My artwork focuses on the intersection of technology, philosophy, science and culture. I use computer code, electronics, kinetic sculpture and painting techniques and materials. You can see the impact that the hotel had on me through my ideas and code sketches and sculpture elements.

Generative window

Generative Window Find your bearings as you gaze through the Generative Window, drawing inspiration from the captivating vista of room 210. This dynamic and interactive artwork captures the essence of the imposing window from room 210, suspended on canvas, serving as both a transformative force and a conduit for expression. Within its frame, a lively fractal tree springs to life, intermingling with elements of the actual view. The scene is further enriched by glimpses of Beijing's bustling streets, featuring cars, pedestrians, and contemporary art pieces seamlessly integrated into the narrative. This multifaceted creation will materialize as an online application leveraging JavaScript, alongside an installation piece displayed on a screen, offering viewers an immersive journey through the intersection of art and technology.

当你凝视着生成之窗，找到你的方向，从210房间的迷人景色中汲取灵感。这个动态交互式艺术作品捕捉了210房间那座宏伟窗户的本质，悬挂在画布上，既是一种转化的力量，也是一种表达的媒介。在它的框架内，一个生动的分形树开始生长，与实际景色的元素交织在一起。场景通过北京繁华的街道的一瞥得到进一步丰富，其中有汽车、行人和当代艺术品，无缝融入叙事之中。这个多层面的创作将以一个在线应用程序的形式实现，利用JavaScript技术，同时还有一个安装作品展示在屏幕上，为观众提供一个沉浸式的艺术与技术交汇之旅。

Shower

This innovative shower, aptly named the Reflection Shower, aims to transcend its utilitarian purpose and elevate the act of cleansing into an experience of profound self-discovery and mental clarity. The shower uses cutting-edge quantum purification technology, in which water molecules are infused with nanomaterials with reflective properties. As these nanomaterials flow through your body, they not only clean up physical impurities, but also trigger a profound introspection that symbolically washes away the myriad possibilities of who you are.

这款创新的淋浴设备，恰如其名的称为“反思淋浴”，旨在超越其实用的目的，将清洁行为提升为一种深刻的自我发现和心智清晰的体验。该淋浴采用尖端的量子净化技术，其中水分子被注入具有反射性质的纳米材料。当这些纳米材料流经您的身体时，它们不仅清洁了身体的杂质，还引发了一种深刻的内省，象征性地洗去了您的多种可能性

Infinite Mirror

Harnessing the captivating illusion of infinite reflection found within the strategically placed bathroom mirrors of the hotel, the Infinity Mirror is a mesmerizing exploration of endless recursion. This optical phenomenon is expertly captured and simulated through an innovative online application developed with JavaScript, alongside an immersive installation piece displayed on a screen controlled by a Windows computer. Both the app and the installation meticulously recreate the mesmerizing effect of the infinity mirror, inviting viewers to lose themselves in the illusion of boundless repetition. Beyond its visual allure, the concept of infinity embedded within the Infinity Mirror serves as a poignant reminder of the profound depths of the unknown. It challenges our perceptions and understanding, compelling us to confront the vastness of existence and the potential insignificance of the individual self within the infinite expanse of possibility. Through this exploration, the Infinity Mirror prompts introspection and contemplation, inviting viewers to grapple with the enigmatic nature of infinity and its implications on our perception of reality.

利用酒店浴室镜子中巧妙设置的引人入胜的无限反射幻觉，无限镜探索了无尽的递归之美。 这一光学现象通过一款创新的JavaScript开发的在线应用程序以及一个由Windows计算机控制的屏幕上的沉浸式装置作品，被巧妙地捕捉和模拟。这两者都精心再现了无限镜的迷人效果，邀请观众沉浸在无尽重复的幻觉中。 除了视觉吸引力之外，无限镜中蕴含的无限概念也是对未知深度的深刻提醒。它挑战了我们的感知和理解，促使我们直面存在的广阔以及在无限可能性的无穷广度中个体自我的潜在微小。 通过这种探索，无限镜引发了内省和沉思，邀请观众探讨无限的神秘本质以及它对我们对现实的感知的影响。

Foam Robot Guest

Foam Robot Guest offers a whimsical exploration of the dynamic relationship between technology and humanity, posing intriguing questions about the future of our coexistence. Amidst concerns about the potential dominance of technology and the rise of artificial intelligence, Foam Robot Guest takes a lighthearted approach to envisioning a world where foam-based automatons occupy spaces traditionally reserved for humans. Their soft, unassuming forms exude playfulness and charm, challenging us to reconsider our perceptions of technological advancement and its implications for society. Through a kinetic sculpture employing servos, sensors, and microcontrollers, this work breathes life into the concept of foam robots as unconventional guests within the hotel space. Accompanied by a video showcasing these endearing robots in various hotel settings, viewers are invited to contemplate the possibility of harmonious coexistence between humans and their technological counterparts. Foam Robot Guest serves as a thought-provoking exploration of the potential futures that lie ahead, sparking conversations about the evolving relationship between humanity and technology in an ever-changing world.

泡沫机器人客人提供了一场诙谐的探索，探讨了技术与人类之间动态关系的奇妙，提出了关于我们共存未来的引人入胜的问题。 在对技术潜在主导和人工智能崛起的担忧中，泡沫机器人客人采取了轻松的方式来想象一个世界，在这个世界里，基于泡沫的自动机占据了传统上为人类保留的空间。它们软软的，朴素的形态散发着俏皮和魅力，挑战我们重新审视技术进步以及其对社会的影响的观念。 通过使用伺服电机、传感器和微控制器的动力雕塑，这项工作为泡沫机器人的概念注入了生命，在酒店空间中成为了非传统的客人。伴随着一段视频，展示了这些可爱的机器人在各种酒店场景中，观众被邀请去思考人类与技术伙伴之间和谐共存的可能性。 泡沫机器人客人作为对未来潜在命运的发人深省的探索，引发了关于人类与技术关系在不断变化的世界中的讨论。

Microscopic Message is a captivating sculptural textile installation that offers a unique exploration into the intricacies of the minuscule world. Utilizing a handheld microscope, this piece delves into the realm of near-microscopic patterns found within items and guests' belongings within the hotel room. By magnifying these minute details, Microscopic Message unveils a hidden tapestry of patterns that might otherwise go unnoticed. These intricate patterns hold the potential to be translated into textile prints, offering a tangible connection between the microscopic and the human scale. Imagining these patterns adorning a bedspread and pillowcase brings the micro-world into the realm of everyday life, allowing us to engage with it in a tangible and meaningful way. Through this exploration of the infinitesimal, Microscopic Message invites viewers to contemplate their place within the vast expanse of the universe. It prompts reflection on how our daily experiences, thoughts, and choices are shaped by the microscopic processes unfolding within us, whether in the intricate workings of our brains or the complex code of our DNA. In unraveling the mysteries of the microscopic world, Microscopic Message offers a profound perspective on the interconnectedness of all things, reminding us of the profound significance embedded within even the smallest of details.

微观信息是一个迷人的雕塑纺织装置，提供了对微小世界复杂性的独特探索。利用手持显微镜，这件作品深入探讨了酒店客房内物品和客人物品中发现的近微观模式的领域。 通过放大这些微小细节，微观信息揭示了一幅隐藏的图案挂毯，否则可能会被忽视。这些复杂的图案有潜力转化为纺织品印花，提供微观和人类尺度之间的有形连接。想象这些图案装饰在床罩和枕套上，将微观世界带入日常生活的领域，使我们能够以有形和有意义的方式与之互动。 通过对微小的探索，微观信息邀请观众思考他们在宇宙广阔空间中的位置。它促使我们反思我们的日常经历、思想和选择如何被我们内在微观过程所塑造，无论是在我们大脑复杂的运作中还是在我们DNA的复杂编码中。 通过揭示微观世界的奥秘，微观信息提供了一个深刻的关于万物相互联系的视角，提醒我们即使在最细微的细节中也蕴含着深远的意义。

Bit Works offers a thought-provoking exploration bridging the realms of biology and technology, revealing the fundamental role of binary code in our modern digital landscape. At its core, Bit Works delves into the underlying deterministic systems present in both biological processes and technological advancements. From the intricate workings of biological systems to the digital tools we interact with daily, everything boils down to the manipulation of bits—ones and zeros—that form the foundation of modern computing. One facet of Bit Works manifests through sculptural installations, featuring colorful boxes arranged in a grid. These boxes encode an 8-byte pattern derived from a conversion of the artist's room number and dates of stay. This fusion of personal data with digital representation not only serves as a visual manifestation of the binary nature of technology but also blurs the lines between the physical and digital realms. Accompanying this sculptural component is a Wolfram cellular automaton visualization of Rule 210, showcasing the deterministic yet unexpectedly complex structures that arise from simple computational rules. This visualization will be accessible both as an online application developed using JavaScript and as an installation piece displayed on a screen equipped with a camera, controlled by a Windows computer. Through Bit Works, viewers are invited to contemplate the intricate interplay between deterministic systems, digital representation, and emergent complexity, prompting reflection on the profound implications of binary code in shaping our understanding of the natural and technological world.

位作品提供了一个引人深思的探索，桥接了生物学和技术领域，揭示了二进制代码在我们现代数字景观中的基础作用。 在其核心，位作品深入探讨了存在于生物过程和技术进步中的基本确定性系统。从生物系统的复杂运作到我们日常互动的数字工具，一切都归结为对位——即1和0——的操纵，这构成了现代计算的基础。 位作品的一个方面通过雕塑装置体现，其中包括排列成网格的色彩丰富的盒子。这些盒子编码了一个8字节的模式，该模式源自对艺术家房间号和入住日期进行转换得到的结果。个人数据与数字表示的融合不仅作为技术的二进制本质的视觉表现，还模糊了物理和数字领域之间的界限。 伴随这个雕塑组件的是对第210号规则的沃尔夫勒姆细胞自动机可视化，展示了由简单计算规则产生的确定性而意想不到的复杂结构。这个可视化将作为一个在线应用程序通过JavaScript开发，也将作为一个装置作品展示在一个装有摄像头的屏幕上，由Windows计算机控制。 通过位作品，观众被邀请思考确定性系统、数字表示和新兴复杂性之间错综复杂的相互作用，进而反思二进制代码在塑造我们对自然和技术世界理解的深远影响。
